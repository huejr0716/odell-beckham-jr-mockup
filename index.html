Odell Beckham Jr is a Wide Receiver in the NFL for the New York Giants. He became a New York Giant in 2014. Today odell is one of the most popular players in the National Football League. In just 3 seasons odell has already broken many NFL records and continues to impress by shredding apart NFL defenses.
